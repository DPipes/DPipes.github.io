Staff Row

Come hang out with us Thursday evenings for a casual live stream. We’ll chat with you in the comments, relive our favorite camp moments, and maybe make a fire, go paddle boarding, or have a pizza night. We just want to catch up with our favorite scouts!

Annie Nyberg
Camp Director:

Annie has been on Owasippe staff since 2013, working as Reneker Family Camp staff and eventually Reneker Family Camp director. She recently completed her bachelor’s degree in Political Science and History and will be attending Law School in the fall. She looks forward to working with all of the scouts and families this summer and cannot wait to see how the staff’s diligent and extensive planning comes to life. She wants to sincerely thank YOU for giving us the opportunity to bring virtual camping to you when you can’t physically be with us.

Dalton DeVries
Program Director:

Dalton started coming to OSR as a scout in 2009, joined staff in 2015, and is now the proud program director of Camp Blackhawk (GO!) and now also Camp O.N. He is majoring in Cinema and Television Arts Production. Outside of camp he has done a lot of (other) strange jobs. Spot him behind the scenes at haunted houses, filming short films, producing tv, and in the background of a Nickelback music video. Populary to contrary belief yellow may not be his favorite color.

Andrew Baert

Katie Johnson

Katie Hogan

Bluegill
Staff:
