Fish and Wildlife Management

In this popular camp badge we’ll learn the importance of our impacts on managing nature. Together we will build bird feeders, learn how to determine the age of fish, and learn how we can make an impact on local species in danger

Workload:

We will construct a wooden bird feeder and create a record of what birds we attract throughout the week and use real fish scales to determine their age. Research career opportunities and how your state wildlife is being managed.

Kit Provides:

Bird feeder kit, string, bird seed, glue, diagram of a fish scale, tree medallion, book of species found at camp

You provide:

Writing utensil and a water cup
