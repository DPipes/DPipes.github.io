Welcome!

Camp O.N. is Pathway to Adventure Council’s virtual summer camp program. Since we are all unable to spend the summer together at camp, we’re bringing camp to you! Whether you are joining us for merit badges and activities or checking out videos and live updates from camp we can’t wait to spend the summer with you. Your whole troop can get involved to earn the Baden Powell award and compete in the camp cup. Use this website as your guide to everything that Owasippe and Napowan have to offer this summer! Check out the campground map and you’ll find lots of activities to participate in. Keep an eye out for new content posted throughout the summer. There are a range of ‘ways to play’ for troops and individuals including free videos to watch, badges to sign up for, hands on projects, and virtual meetings to join. View the schedule to see the important dates and mark down the events you don't want to miss.


What you get as a week long camper:

  • Four merit badges with supplies
  • Five cooking activities
  • A craft, a science experiment, AND a VR headset project
  • Additional online activities and content, like game night or live streams with the staff
  • Owasippe or Napowan 2020 patch, shirt, and backpack

You can also register and participate in any badge or activity individually.


Ways to participate as a troop:

  • Earn the Baden Powell Award
  • Compete against other troops in the Camp Cup Competition
  • Be featured in our weekly newspaper
  • Attend SPL meetings and adult leader meetings to see familiar faces and learn more about Camp O.N.
  • Owasippe or Napowan 2020 patch, shirt, and backpack
